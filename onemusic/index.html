Rhapsody is an innovative solution which aims to target Music professionals, composers, and instrumentalists by providing them with modern AI-driven tools to experiment with synthetic music generation. Rhapsody is developed keeping in mind the cutting-edge research and trends with an objective to provide with a seamless experience and value in terms of quality music generation. Rhapsody is a desktop application which helps musicians and other music enthusiasts by delivering them with a toolkit to experiment with musical tunes and synthetic audio with the use of AI.
